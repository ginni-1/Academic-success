ROADMAP & STRATEGY




        FOR ACADEMIC PREPERATION

  1. 🙂
    Maintain Proper Notes
    Know your syllabus and weightage of each topic thoroughly. Maintain proper notes according to it.
  2. 😀
    Plan Your Studies Effectively
    Create a study plan.Study from different websites like 'GeeksforGeeks' ,and youtube lectures for different topics.
  3. 😄
    Solve Previous Year Ques.Papers
    Mostly PYQ's repeat in university exam . So, You can find previous year question papers on the university website or in the library.
  4. 😁
    Important Topics Before The Exam
    In the days leading up to the exam, focus on revising the most important topics. You can use your notes, textbooks, and practice problems to revise.




        FOR DEVELOPING SKILLS

  1. 🙂
    Identify your target skills
    Some common CSE skills include: Programming languages (e.g., Python, Java, C++, JavaScript),
    Data structures and algorithms, Software engineering principles, Databases, Computer networking,
    Operating systems, Artificial intelligence, Machine learning.
  2. 😀
    Follow these Roadmaps
    Roadmap is very necessary to track your lkearning and be systeematic in particular course.Explore and select the
    Roadmap of your target skill. "Click here".
  3. 😄
    Find learning resources
    There are many different ways to learn CSE skills. You can take online courses, read books and articles, watch video tutorials,
    or attend workshops and conferences. Some popular online learning platforms for CSE include: W3School, mdn, Coursera, edX,
    Udacity, Udemy, Codecademy. Apart from these you can also search youtube tutorials .
    ƒ
  4. 😁
    Set realistic goals and be consistent
    Don't try to learn too many skills at once. Focus on one or two skills at a time, and set realistic goals for yourself. For example, you might set
    a goal to learn the basics of Python in one month. Practice regularly. The best way to learn CSE skills is to practice regularly. Try to set aside
    some timeeach day or week to practice your new skills. You might start by working on coding challenges or building small projects.




        FOR INTERNSHIP /JOB APPLICATION

  1. 🙂
    Identify the skills and experience required for the internship/job
    Read the job description carefully and identify the skills and experience that the employer is looking for.
    Make a list of your own skills and experience, and highlight any that are relevant to the job.
  2. 😀
    Tailor your resume and cover letter to each job you apply for
    Your resume and cover letter should be tailored to each specific job you apply for. Highlight your most relevant skills
    and experience, and explain how they make you a good fit for the job.
  3. 😄
    Practice your interviewing skills
    One of the best ways to prepare for an interview is to practice. Practice answering common interview questions. Online interview
    preparation resources like Google Career Center Interview Preparation Guide,Interview Cake,CareerCup may help.
  4. 😁
    Follow up after your interviews.
    After your interviews, be sure to send a thank-you note to each interviewer within 24 hours. This shows that you are appreciative of their time
    and consideration.
